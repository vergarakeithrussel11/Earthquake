HAZARD MAP

Hazard maps offer crucial information that can be used to reduce catastrophe risk and assist people comprehend the dangers of natural hazards.

                          

EARTHQUAKE HAZARD MAP

The term "earthquake hazard," often known as "seismic hazard," refers to the amount of ground shaking anticipated as a result of future potential earthquakes. Hazard maps show the size of anticipated risk areas and can be used in conjunction with disaster management data, such as locations for evacuation and evacuation routes.
