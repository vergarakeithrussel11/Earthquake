What to Do, Before, During, and After an Earthquake

Knowing what to do before, during, and after a natural disaster will greately help you and the people around you get away from danger.

                 

WHAT TO DO BEFORE!

  • Check your surroundings if there is any damage that can potentially harm you.

  • Turn on radio, or check the news for any updates.

  • Be mindful for potential aftershocks

                 

WHAT TO DO DURING!

  • The most important to do during earthquake is to STAY CALM!

  • DROP, COVER, AND HOLD!

  • Stay away from tall buildings

  • If you're driving, stop the car and stay inside.

                 

WHAT TO DO AFTER!

  • Identify what other potential dangers that can occur in your area.

  • Have access to emergency service phone numbers.

  • Create an escape route from your house.

  • Prepare a first aid kit.
